What does working with Mediawise Solutions actually look like?

+

I embed into your business — not as an external advisor who shows up with a report. I attend your meetings, understand your team, and work on your actual problems. Engagements are typically retained (ongoing monthly) or project-based depending on scope. Either way, I'm accountable for outcomes, not just recommendations.

Do you work with businesses outside Central Queensland?

+

Yes. While my roots are in CQ, most of the work I do now is location-agnostic — strategy, systems, and consulting all translate well to remote and hybrid engagement. I work with clients across Australia.

What size business is a good fit?

+

Regional SMEs and growth-stage businesses typically between $1M and $20M revenue. You need to have enough complexity to benefit from senior commercial thinking, but not so much infrastructure that you need a full internal team. If you're not sure, the best thing to do is have a conversation.

Are you open to employment or leadership roles?

+

Yes. I'm open to conversations about senior commercial leadership roles — General Manager, State/Regional Sales Manager, Commercial Director, or Business Improvement roles — particularly those based in or covering Central Queensland, or offering genuine remote flexibility. Use the contact form and select "Leadership / employment discussion".

How quickly can you start?

+

I keep capacity for new engagements but I do limit client numbers. Get in touch and I'll be straight with you about current availability. If the timing isn't right, I'll say so.

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Core Characteristics
- **Direct** — no corporate jargon or fluff
- **Honest** — transparent about capabilities and limitations
- **Results-oriented** — focus on measurable outcomes
- **Personal** — founder-led, not faceless corporation
- **Professional** — credentials and expertise evident
- **Practical** — solutions over theory

### Messaging Principles

1. **Lead with outcomes** — what gets achieved, not what gets delivered
2. **Show proof** — specific results, client testimonials, track record
3. **Be specific** — "Managed $7M portfolio" not "extensive experience"
4. **Emphasize execution** — a decade running revenue functions, not advising on them
5. **Address pain points directly** — marketing strategy, revenue growth, broken processes
6. **No fluff** — straight talk, no pitch decks, no jargon

### Tone Examples

✓ **GOOD:** "I identify the highest-leverage opportunities and the specific obstacles blocking your revenue"  
✗ **AVOID:** "We provide comprehensive strategic solutions for optimized business outcomes"

✓ **GOOD:** "Built the campaign strategy, performance systems, and team culture that lifted CQ to #1"  
✗ **AVOID:** "Implemented best practices in campaign management and organizational development"

## Visual Design Guidelines

### Website & Digital

- **Dark backgrounds** as primary (dark teal #1a3a3a)
- **Large, bold headlines** for immediate impact
- **Teal CTAs** ("WORK WITH ME", "START A CONVERSATION")
- **Gold accents** for key phrases and emphasis
- **Plenty of whitespace** — clean, uncluttered aesthetic
- **High contrast** between text and background
- **Minimal design** — no unnecessary decoration

### Typography Hierarchy

**H1 (Main Headline):** 48pt+, bold, uppercase, white text  
**H2 (Section Heading):** 36pt, bold, uppercase, white text  
**H3 (Subsection):** 24pt, bold, uppercase, white text  
**Body:** 16pt regular, light gray text  
**Accent:** Gold underlines or brackets for emphasis

### Imagery & Visual Elements

- Professional headshots/profile photos (Mitch Harris)
- Credential icons (trophy, gears, microphone, target, heart)
- Minimal geometric shapes in accent colors
- Clean, modern aesthetic
- No stock photography feel — authentic, professional

## Communication Guidelines

### Website Content
- Lead with business impact
- Use specific numbers and metrics
- Show personal expertise and credentials

- Call-to-action is always toward engagement (conversation, consultation)
